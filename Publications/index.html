ArcUser Magazine

During my work as a GIS Analyst, I was taking care of a large amount of feature layers, which contained a lot of attributes. While looking for a way to automate most of my updates, I came across the Attribute Assistant Add-Inn. You can download this Add-Inn here.

After using this Add-Inn for some time, I have noticed that none of my colleagues knew about this Add-Inn. This inspired me to write an article for the ESRI ArcUser magazine, which talked about my experience using this Add-Inn. You can find this article here.
